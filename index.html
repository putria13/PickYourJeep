JEEP® OFFERS THE ULTIMATE ADVENTURE IN INDONESIA

Jeep is a car company that produces a variety of SUV car products. This car company was founded in 1941 and has been part of Chrysler since 1987.
Its head office is located in Toledo, Ohio. Jeep is also a division of FCA US LLC, a subsidiary of Fiat Chrysler Automobiles. Jeep has been part of Chrysler
since 1987 when the Jeep brand was owned by Chrysler along with other assets from its former owner, American Motors Corporation (AMC). Jeep's
products range in the SUV segment both crossovers and off-road SUVs. Besides that. Jeep also produces a truck, several small vans and a roadster.

Over the years, the term Jeep has continued to be associated as an umbrella term that refers to vehicles that can be relied on for off-road use which of
course has difficult terrain. The off-road capabilities of the Jeep production cars, which cannot be doubted for their reliability, have made this vehicle
have its fans in various parts of the world.
